Golden Gamble Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Data We Collect

None from you. Your Gold Balance and game progress are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads. When you watch a rewarded ad or purchase Gold:

  • AdMob may collect your Advertising ID (IDFA) and device info to serve relevant ads.
  • This is used for ad personalization and analytics by Google.
  • You control this: iOS Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking.

See Google’s Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Your Rights

  • Delete the app → all local data (Gold Balance) is gone.
  • Opt out of ad personalization in iOS Settings.
